About Me

I work as a reservoir engineer in a depletion management squad focused on managing the decline of the gas field safely and effectively. Furthermore, I work on evaluating wellwork options, infill wells, and near field exploration wells aimed at increasing the life of the field.
To accomplish this I use both classical and deterministic methods aligned with appropriate subsurface, and steel assumptions as well as bespoke and self-made probabilistic methods to incorporate the appropriate range of uncertainty. The probabilistic methods are classical methods adjusted to be run as monte carlo simulations and integration with industry-standard software.
Outside of work I enjoy creating personal coding projects such as building video games, making music, and engaging in various sports, my favourite being football.
